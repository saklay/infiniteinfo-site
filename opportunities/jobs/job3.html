Project Manager

Responsibilities:

  • Coordinate with sales, assess client requirements, develop project schedules.
  • Assemble and lead project teams, assign and manage project tasks to completion.
  • Manage the client relationship: coordinate project schedules with the client, communicate project status to the client, provide general customer support.

Job Qualifications:

  • BA degree in mass communications, advertising or business, or related experience.
  • Strong written and oral English.
  • Good "people" skills.
  • Good organizational skills.
  • Prior project management experience a plus, but not necessary. We will train chosen candidate.
  • Prior experience in advertising or multimedia production a plus, but not necessary.
