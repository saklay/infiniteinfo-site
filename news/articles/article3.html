September 18, 1998

Infinite Named Webby Award Finalist

www.Whilce.com was selected as one of the five finalists under the Arts Category of The Philippine Webby Awards.

Designed for Whilce Portacio, the Philippines' most famous comic book artist, this site promotes his latest work called Stone which features characters derived from Philippine folklore doing battle against each other in a quest for ultimate power.

Check out the super cool Shockwave-powered character database, Wheel of Power, and the incomparable Shadow Worlds virtual reality explorer.
