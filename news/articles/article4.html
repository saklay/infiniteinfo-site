April 1, 1998

Infinite Opens U.S. Office

Taking our first step towards achieving our goal of becoming one of the world's leading interactive media providers, Infinite Information America, LLC opened its doors this April.

Located in the multimedia gulch, south of Market Street, San Francisco, Infinite America will be responsible for sales, marketing and project coordination in the United States.

The new company is headed by Paul Cicerone, who can be reached at:

Infinite is an interactive media company whose production facility (locally, the largest and fastest growing) is strategically located in the Philippines, giving corporations an opportunity to have all of their interactive media applications produced at the highest quality for less investment than is possible anywhere in the world. The key for Infinite is satisfying our customers, innovating and keeping prices low.
