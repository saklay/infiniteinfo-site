We deliver our projects on time due to our structured project methodology (separating content from programming) and our deep resource pool. Our company has a highly qualified staff of over 30 people who can serve you. We are organized into the following teams:
  • Executive Management
  • Creative Engineering
  • Sales
  • Marketing
  • Operations
  • MIS

Our Creative Engineering Team is organized into Creative, Programming and Project Management Groups. The Creative Group handles all creative and design aspects of the project including:

  • Graphics
  • Animation
  • 3D design
  • Audio editing
  • Video editing

The Programming Group handles:

  • Interactive programming
  • Database programming
  • Application programming
  • Systems programming

The groups work in parallel to reduce application development time. The separation gives our staff the luxury of being totally focused on their respective talents. It also gives them more time for research and development.

Working closely with the groups is Project Management. Project Management functions as the single point of contact for our customers post sales. They:

  • Monitor project progress
  • Coordinate materials
  • Conduct product and project reviews
  • Make schedules and ensure deadline compliance
  • Ensure quality control

Our Sales Team is comprised of our interactive media consultants; they sell and provide killer customer service. They are driven by the desire to identify opportunities where Infinite can add value to information. Once identified, their function is to help companies realize this value through a consultative sales approach.

The Marketing team supports the Sales team. They increase our sales effectivity by providing information that we need to make better decisions. The Marketing team helps generate leads and track our competitors. They provide sales ammunition through training and documentation, produce our collateral including CD-ROMs, brochures, diskette presentations and our web site. They also manage public relations both locally, internationally and online. They are also available to help our customers with their interactive marketing requirements.

Our Operations team handles our accounting, payroll, HR, office management, finance, collections, purchasing, asset management (furniture, plant, property, equipment), reporting, maintenance and cleaning.

Our MIS team handles our SKYWAY project (building and improving the technical infrastructure of the company), networking, our intranet and extranet, software, software licenses and registration, backups, custom kiosk setup, hardware and equipment, exhibit setup and technical issues, furniture, communications, e-mail/mail servers, file systems and performance support systems.
